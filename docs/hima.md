# Hima — Product Overview

> Last updated: March 2026

## What is Hima

Hima is a social networking platform that connects **female creators** with **male users** through paid voice and video calls. Users purchase coins to initiate calls with creators, while chat is available for free. The platform operates primarily in South India.

---

## How It Works

### For Users (Male)
- Browse and discover verified female creators
- Chat with creators for free
- Purchase coins to make **voice calls** and **video calls**
- Coin pricing: **₹25 = 40 coins**
- Average spend per user: **~₹150**

### For Creators (Female)
- Onboarded through **manual verification** (staff listens to audio and calls them to confirm identity)
- Earn payouts from calls made by users
- Creators may also work on competing/multiple platforms (non-exclusive)

---

## Revenue Model

| Component        | Share  |
|------------------|--------|
| Creator Payout   | 34%    |
| GST              | 18%    |
| **Hima Profit**  | **48%** |

### Unit Economics (per ₹100 user spend)

- ₹34 goes to the creator
- ₹18 goes to GST
- ₹48 is Hima's gross margin
