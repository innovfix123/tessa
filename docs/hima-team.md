## Operations Team

Reports to: **Bala (COO)**

| Person | Role | Responsibility |
|--------|------|----------------|
| **Sneha Sunoj** | Operations Manager | Manages the entire support operation for Hima. Monitors daily ticket resolution, creator onboarding quality, SLA compliance, and app performance. Escalation point for all regional support issues. |
| Ranjini | Tamil Support Representative | Handles user/creator tickets in Tamil. Assists with creator onboarding and verification calls for Tamil-speaking creators. |
| Gousia | Telugu Support Representative | Handles user/creator tickets in Telugu. Assists with creator onboarding and verification calls for Telugu-speaking creators. |
| Deeksha | Kannada Support Representative | Handles user/creator tickets in Kannada. Assists with creator onboarding and verification calls for Kannada-speaking creators. |
| Reshma | Malayalam Support Representative | Handles user/creator tickets in Malayalam. Assists with creator onboarding and verification calls for Malayalam-speaking creators. |

### What Operations Does

- **User support**: Resolve complaints, refund requests, technical issues
- **Creator onboarding**: Verify identity through audio check + phone call, approve creator profiles
- **Creator management**: Handle payout queries, deactivation, re-verification
- **Daily monitoring**: Track ticket volumes, resolution rates, SLA adherence
- **Escalation**: Critical issues go to Sneha → Bala → CEO

### Key Metrics Tracked

- Daily ticket volume and resolution rate
- Average resolution time
- Creator onboarding funnel (applications → verified → active)
- User complaints by category
- SLA compliance %

## Marketing & Content Team

Reports to: **Nandha (CMO + BLR PM)**

| Person | Role | Scope | Responsibility |
|--------|------|-------|----------------|
| **Anirudh** | Performance Marketing | Hima only | Manages all paid advertising — Google Ads and Meta Ads. Controls budgets, optimizes campaigns, tracks ROAS and CPA. Sole person responsible for paid user acquisition. |
| **Anindita** | Growth Manager, North India | Hima only | Responsible for creative strategy to expand Hima into North India. Delivers creative concepts and campaign ideas to Nandha's team for execution. Identifies influencers and creators in Hindi-speaking markets. Does not run ads — provides the creative direction. |
| **Laxmi** | Social Media Manager | Hima only | Manages all Hima social media accounts. Posts content, tracks engagement, builds organic reach and follower growth across platforms. |
| **Krishnan** | Content Lead | Hima only | Creates scripts, generates AI video ideas, manages the content calendar. Oversees Disha's output. Responsible for content that drives organic growth. |
| **Disha** | Content Creator | Hima only | Creates content pieces (videos, posts) based on Krishnan's scripts and direction. Reports to Krishnan. |
| **Anaz** | Video Editor | Primarily Hima | Edits video content for Hima campaigns, social media, and ads. May handle urgent requests from other apps when needed. |
| **Sooraj** | Graphic Designer | Primarily Hima | Creates ad creatives, social media graphics, banners, and thumbnails for Hima. May handle other app requests when capacity allows. |
| **Tiyasa** | Content Creator | Bangalore Connect | Creates content for BLR-focused campaigns and Bangalore Connect initiatives. |
| **Maansi** | Content Creator | Bangalore Connect | Produces BLR campaign content and supports social/video publishing needs. |

### How Marketing Works for Hima

1. **Paid acquisition** (Anirudh): Meta Ads is the sole user acquisition channel. Anirudh manages budgets, creates campaigns, A/B tests audiences and creatives.
2. **Creative pipeline**: Anaz makes video ads, Sooraj designs static creatives → Anirudh runs them → performance data flows back to optimize.
3. **Organic content** (Krishnan + Disha + Laxmi): Scripts → videos → published on social media. Laxmi manages distribution and engagement.
4. **North India expansion** (Anindita): Researches market, provides creative concepts → Nandha approves → Anirudh tests them as ads.

### Key Metrics Tracked

- Weekly ad spend and ROAS
- CPA (cost per acquisition)
- New installs from paid channels
- Content pieces published per week
- Social media reach, engagement rate, follower growth
- North India creative hit rate (concepts that achieve 3x+ ROAS)

## Cross-Team Workflow

```
User issue reported
    → Support (Sneha's team) resolves or escalates
    → Bug? → Bala → Yuvanesh → Rishabh fixes → Raksha tests → deploy
    → Feature request? → CEO → Yuvanesh → Rishabh builds → Raksha tests

Ad campaign needs creative
    → Anirudh requests → Anaz (video) or Sooraj (design) creates
    → Anirudh runs the ad → tracks performance

North India expansion
    → Anindita delivers creative concepts → Nandha approves
    → Anirudh tests as paid ads → reports results back

Content pipeline
    → Krishnan writes scripts → Disha creates content
    → Anaz edits video → Laxmi publishes on social media
```

## Important Notes

- **Rishabh is a single point of failure** for Hima engineering. If he is unavailable, there is no backup developer (Barkha is an intern).
- **Anaz and Sooraj** are primarily Hima but may get pulled for other apps. If Hima creative work is delayed, check if they have cross-app requests.
- **Anindita does not execute** — she provides creative ideas and strategy. Nandha's team (Anirudh, Anaz, Sooraj) does the execution.
- **No dedicated Hima Product Manager** exists. Product decisions flow from CEO → relevant department head.
- **Co-founders**: JP (CEO) and Ayush (CFO).
